# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-03-13

---

## 1. Introduction

DateSense respects your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what data DateSense processes when you use the app.

DateSense does not collect or transmit your private self-reflection content, personal notes, answers, or relationship entries to our servers for storage or analysis. That content remains on your device.

To provide account access, app reliability, analytics, and subscription functionality, DateSense does process limited account, technical, and usage-related data as described below.

---

## 2. Data We Process

DateSense may process the following categories of data:

- email address or private relay email address provided through Sign in with Apple;
- authentication-related data required for account registration and login;
- diagnostic data, such as crash reports;
- app analytics data, such as screens viewed, buttons tapped, and general feature usage;
- subscription and purchase-related data required to support in-app purchases and subscriptions.

DateSense does not process your private notes, self-reflections, questionnaire answers, or relationship records on external servers for analytics or profiling.

---

## 3. How We Use Data

We use the limited data described above only to:

- create and manage your account;
- allow you to sign in securely;
- maintain app stability and fix crashes;
- understand general app usage and improve features;
- support subscriptions and purchase-related functionality.

We do not sell your personal data.
We do not use your data for third-party advertising.
We do not use your private in-app content for marketing.

---

## 4. Storage and Processing

Your private app content, including notes, reflections, answers, and relationship-related entries, is stored locally on your device.

Limited account, analytics, diagnostic, and subscription-related data may be processed by trusted service providers only to support app functionality.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

DateSense may use third-party service providers for purposes such as:

- authentication;
- crash reporting;
- app analytics;
- subscription and purchase support.

These services process only the data necessary to perform their function.

---

## 6. Data Control

You remain in control of your data.

Within the app, you may be able to:

- delete locally stored app data on your device;
- delete your account;
- log out of your account;
- protect access using a PIN code.

Deleting the app removes locally stored app content from your device, but it does not automatically delete your account.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

DateSense is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe that a child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review and address the issue.

---

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be published at this location. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised Privacy Policy.
